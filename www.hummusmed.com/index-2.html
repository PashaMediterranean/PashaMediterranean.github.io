ABOUT US

If you’re craving Mediterranean comfort food, you will love Hummus Mediterranean Restaurant. Located at 150 E. Fourth Avenue in San Mateo. We serve fresh, authentic Mediterranean, Lebanese and Turkish dishes cooked-to-order. We pride ourselves on our fresh ingredients and fragrant herbs and spices and creating succulent food you will return for again and again.

We have Hummus in our name for a reason – we take our hummus seriously! That’s why we created six flavored versions of our creamy chickpea dip. You can choose our earthy Pesto hummus, or spicy Chipotle hummus, Sweet and Spicy hummus with pine nuts and harissa sauce, marinated Artichoke hummus, Avocado hummus with cumin and garlic, or our hummus topped with grilled mushrooms. Don’t forget to scoop your hummus up with our house-baked pita bread.

MORE INFO

For a light lunch, why not start with a salad? Try our unique roasted beet salad – earthy roasted beets mingling with candied walnuts, parmesan cheese and a cherry vinaigrette. Our authentic , tangy Lebanese Tabbouleh salad is made from bulgur wheat tossed with freshly chopped parsley, mint, tomatoes, cucumbers and onion, and is a perfect accompaniment for any of our entrees.

We offer Falafel with Babaganoush as a house plate, as well as other delicious entrees such as a 7-vegetable tagine with fresh vegetables, kalamata olives, apricots and a chermoula sauce, or our vegetarian Moussaka, filled with layers of eggplant, yellow squash, a creamy béchamel sauce and a side of couscous. If you are in the mood for American-inspired comfort food, try our Falafel Burger with avocado hummus spread and piled high with lettuce, pickles, onions, drizzled with tahini and served on lavash bread and a side of French fries.

From hot and cold mezze to gyros and flatbreads, we’re on a mission to expose the Bay Area to the diverse and rich flavors and aromas of Mediterranean.
