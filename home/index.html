ABOUT US

If you’re craving Mediterranean comfort food, you will love Pasha Mediterranean Restaurant. Located in Downtown Redwood City, We serve fresh, authentic Mediterranean, Lebanese and Turkish dishes cooked-to-order. We pride ourselves on our fresh ingredients and fragrant herbs and spices and creating succulent food you will return for again and again.

From Istanbul to California, Serkan Bikim has had one vision only: To provide the people of California with authentic, delicious and award winning Turkish and Mediterranean food that warms the heart and fills the stomach to perfection. He is the proud owner of Hummus Mediterranean in Downtown San Mateo and his success has landed him a new, beautiful restaurant in the delightful, Downtown Redwood City. Pasha Med is ready to feed your soul with healthy food and our doors are open to new friends, so ask for Serkan anytime. Thank you and Enjoy!
